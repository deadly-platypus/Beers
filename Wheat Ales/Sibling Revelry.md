Sibling Revelry
===============

Ingredients
-----------

Grain
-----
1.	5 	lbs	German Pilsner
1.	5 	lbs	American 2-Row
1.	2	lbs	American Wheat
1.	10.2	ozs	Flaked Rye

Hops
----
1.	.58	ozs	Centennial
1.	2.58	ozs	Amarillo
1.	.77	ozs	Citra

Spices
------
1.	.24	ozs	Parsley
1.	.09	ozs	Rosemary
1.	.09	ozs	Lemon Thyme
1.	.04	ozs	White Sage

Yeast
-----
1.	Wyeast 3711

Mashing
-------
1.	4 gallons @ 163F -> 149F for 1 hour
1.	Raise to 160F
1.	Sparge 5 gallons @ 168F

Boil
----
1.	Centennial @ 60 minutes
1.	Whirlfloc @ 15 minutes
1.	Amarillo @ 5 minutes
1.	Spices @ 5 minutes

Dry Hop
-------
1. Citra @ 2 weeks
